How to Play Mancala

Objective

Collect as many stones as possible in your bank by the end of the game.

Setup

The board has 12 small pits (6 for each player) and 2 banks (one for each player). Each small pit starts with 4 stones.

Gameplay

  1. On your turn, pick one of your pits with stones.
  2. Pick up all stones from that pit and distribute them one by one into subsequent pits in a counter-clockwise direction, including your bank but skipping your opponent's bank.
  3. If your last stone lands in your bank, you get another turn.
  4. If your last stone lands in an empty pit on your side and the opposite pit has stones, you capture those stones plus your last stone into your bank.
  5. The game ends when all pits on one side are empty. The player with stones remaining gets to put them in their bank.

Winning

The player with the most stones in their bank at the end wins!
